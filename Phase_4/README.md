# Phase 4: Testing, Pivot Strategy, & Validation (Months 7 & 8)

The final phase highlights engineering adaptability. Facing supply chain issues, the team successfully pivoted the testing methodology from aquatic to terrestrial environments, rigorously validating the underlying mechanical principles.

## 📂 Directory Structure

* **`/Testing_Data`**: Logs and notes on locomotion speed, power draw during actuation, and structural stress observation.
* **`/Code`**: Final, tuned actuation scripts adjusted for the increased friction and resistance of solid-ground movement.
* **`/Images_and_Video`**: Media demonstrating the physical prototype successfully executing sinusoidal locomotion across land.

## 🎯 Phase Milestones Achieved

1. **Strategic Pivot:** Due to unforeseen procurement issues with essential marine-grade waterproofing components, the team adapted the testing parameters to evaluate the robot entirely on land.
2. **Terrestrial Validation:** Successfully demonstrated that the undulating fin propulsion mechanism, powered by the gym mat and linkage arms, could generate forward thrust on a solid surface.
3. **Performance Analysis:** Validated the structural integrity of the 3D-printed chassis and the power delivery of the dual-battery system under high-friction load, gathering vital performance data for future amphibious iterations.
